Welcome to Happy Pets!

The first social media to share everything about your pets.

This is a simple application that allows you to create a profile for your pet, find friends, and chat with them.


Features:

  • Sign up and log in
  • Create a profile for your pets
  • Find friends
  • Chat with your friends
  • Create walks in your neighborhood
  • Share memorable moments (coming later)
